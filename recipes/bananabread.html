Chocolate Lava Cake Recipe

Description

This recipe involves very little time and is perfect for a nutrisous snack for an afternoon in the office.

The most important part of makig a delicious recipe is the ingredients. Make sure to prioritize .

Ingredients:
  • 1 hearty slice of sourdough
  • 1/2 a banana, sliced
  • 2 heaping tablespoons of peanut butter
Steps:
  1. Toast bread until edges are crisp
  2. Spread peanut butter evenly across
  3. Strategically place bananas to ensure a perfect ratio
