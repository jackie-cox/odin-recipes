Chocolate Lava Cake Recipe

Description

This recipe involves an easy air fryer tactic that minimize dishes and the chance for failure. I have made many lava cake recipes before and they are either too difficult or require equipment I do not own.

The most important part of makig a delicious recipe is the ingrediets. Make sure to prioritize fresh eggs and quality chocolate as those are carry the most prominance in both flavor and texture.

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 stick of butter
  • as much chocolate to make heart happy
  • enough flour to make rise
  • salt to taste
  • 3 eggs of the preferred size
Steps:
  1. Measure out all ingredient after eggs get to room temperature
  2. Melt the butter in the microwave in 30s increments
  3. Add chocolate into melted butter and whisk until combine
  4. Crack eggs into seperate bowl and add chcolate-butter mix a little at a time until fully combined
  5. Add all of dry ingredients
  6. Bake in over at 350 degrees for 12 minutes
