Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Description

This recipe involves an easy microwave tactic for cooking eggs. This super quick and easy recipe also reduces dishes used for quick clean up.

The most important part of making a delicious recipe is the ingredients. Make sure to prioritize farm fresh eggs if attainable.

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 stick of butter
  • salt to taste
  • 3 eggs of the preferred size
Steps:
  1. Measure out all ingredient after eggs get to room temperature
  2. Melt the butter in the microwave in 30s increments
  3. Crack eggs into same bowl
  4. Microwave for 1 minute, stir, and repeat once again
